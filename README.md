🎬 Creative Portfolio Website

A modern portfolio website created for content creators and video editors to showcase their work, skills, and creative journey in a professional and visually appealing way.

📌 About The Project

This portfolio website serves as a digital space where creators can present their editing projects, highlight their services, and share their latest work with potential clients and collaborators.

The goal of the website is to provide an engaging platform that reflects creativity while making it easy for visitors to explore projects, view recent videos, and get in touch.
